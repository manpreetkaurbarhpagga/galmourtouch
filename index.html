About Us

Creating Beauty Since 2012

Glamour Touch was founded with a vision to transform the makeup experience from a service into an art form. Our founder, Alicia Monroe, started with a single chair in her apartment and has grown Glamour Touch into the premier makeup destination in the city.

We believe makeup is more than just cosmetics—it's confidence, expression, and empowerment. Our talented artists are trained in the latest techniques and committed to using only the highest quality, cruelty-free products.

Whether you're preparing for your wedding day, a special event, or just want to elevate your everyday look, we're here to create magic together.
